Clarus Navy

Use for headers, navigation, primary buttons, and key structural elements. Should feel authoritative and grounding.

Clarus White

Dominates content areas, creates breathing room. The canvas that lets other elements stand out.

Clarus Gold

Maximum 5% of any layout. Reserved exclusively for Index scores, premium badges, and key highlights. Overuse diminishes impact.

Slate + Stone

Supporting roles only. Slate for secondary text and metadata. Stone for subtle backgrounds and dividers.

Key Color Usage Rules

  1. Navy and White must dominate - Together they should comprise 80%+ of any layout
  2. Gold is rare and meaningful - Maximum 5% of any layout; used only for scores and premium highlights
  3. Never use more than 3 colors in any single component or section
  4. Avoid gradients except for subtle depth on interactive elements
  5. Maintain contrast ratios - 4.5:1 minimum for body text, 3:1 for large text
