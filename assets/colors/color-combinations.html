Approved Color Combinations & Contrast Ratios

WCAG Accessibility Requirements

  • AAA (7:1+) - Enhanced accessibility, optimal for body text
  • AA (4.5:1+) - Minimum for body text and small UI elements
  • AA Large (3:1+) - Acceptable for large text (18px+ or 14px bold)

Combinations to Avoid

Do not use: Gold on White (2.44:1), Stone on White (1.28:1), or any combination with a contrast ratio below 3:1. These fail accessibility requirements and compromise readability.
